Data Visualization Final Project

This was submitted as the final project of a Data Visualization class at Johns Hopkins in Spring 2016.

The United Nations Development Program (UNDP) uses the Human Development Index (HDI) as a crude measurement of a country's development. The scale is from 0 to 1, with 1 being the highest. It is simply the geometric mean of 3 indices: a country's life expectancy, overall years spent in education, and Gross National Income (GNI).

Governance Indicators

The World Bank publishes Worldwide Governance Indicators (WGI) to measure and compare the effectiveness of government institutions. Measures are on a scale of -2.5 to 2.5, but have been shifted to 0 to 5 for this visualization. These cover the period 1996 to 2014.

Source: World Bank
